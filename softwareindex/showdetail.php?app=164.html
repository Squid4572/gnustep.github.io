Zipper - published: 21 Apr 2013

Archive extraction and creation utility - by Dirk Olmes

Zipper is a tool for inspecting the contents of a compressed archive and for extracting it. Its GUI is inspired by WinZip. It supports tar, gz, ZIP, and many more. It integrates with GWorkspace services for the creation of archives.
